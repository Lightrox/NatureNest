__Color Palette

Background:
Soft Cream → #FFF8E7
(A gentle, clean background that feels modern and welcoming)

Header & Footer:
Deep Teal → #2C7A7B
White Text → #FFFFFF
(Professional yet friendly; ensures text is readable and links pop)

Product Card Background:
White → #FFFFFF
Light Shadow → rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.08)
(Keeps products crisp, fresh, and slightly elevated)

Price Text:
Rich Copper → #B65E34
Charcoal Gray → #2E2E2E
(Elegant contrast for text, communicates quality and trustworthiness)

Amazon/Flipkart Buttons:
Deep Teal → #2C7A7B (for Amazon button)
Sunset Coral → #FF6F61 (for Flipkart button or “View More” CTA)
(Bold but not overpowering; draws attention to actionable elements)

Accent / Hover Color:
Soft Mint → #A8E6CF
(Use for hover effects or small highlights; adds freshness and modern vibe)
