___Color Palets___
Background:

Warm Beige → #F5F1E6
(Soft, natural tone for the main page background)

Header & Footer:

Olive Green → #6B8E23

White Text → #FFFFFF
(Gives a grounded, nature-first feel while keeping text readable)

Product Card Background:

White → #FFFFFF

Light Shadow → rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)
(Keeps products clean and fresh-looking)

Price Text:

Earth Brown → #8B5E3C

Charcoal Gray → #333333
(Elegant and easy on the eyes, maintains trustworthiness)

Amazon/Flipkart Buttons:

Olive Green → #6B8E23 (for Amazon button)

Soft Coral → #E07A5F (for Flipkart button or “View More” CTA)

___Directory Format___

health-products-site/
│
├── index.html                # Homepage with quote + featured products
├── face.html                 # Face category page
├── food.html                 # Food category page
├── body.html                 # Body category page
├── about.html                # About Us page
│
├── css/
│   └── style.css             # Main styles
│
├── js/
│   └── script.js             # Main JS for rendering products
│
├── data/
│   └── products.json         # All product details
│
├── images/
│   ├── face-cream.webp
│   ├── honey.webp
│   ├── body-lotion.webp
│   └── ...                   # All product images
│
└── assets/
    ├── logo.png              # Logo or icons
    └── favicon.ico           # Browser tab icon
